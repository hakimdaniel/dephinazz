About Dephinazz

Our Mission 

To enhance everyday living by providing high-quality, reliable, and innovative home appliances that bring comfort, convenience, and efficiency to every household. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and empowering families with products that simplify their lives and create a more enjoyable home environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of home appliances, recognized for our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where every home is equipped with smart, energy-efficient appliances that contribute to a healthier and more connected world, enhancing the quality of life for families everywhere.

History

Founded in 2024 by Glenn and his partners, Hakim and Hazrie, HomeEase Appliances began in Permatang Pauh, Pulau Pinang, with a mission to provide high-quality home essentials. Starting with washing machines, televisions, and air conditioners, the team quickly gained a reputation for delivering reliable and innovative products. From the outset, HomeEase focused on sustainability by introducing eco-friendly, energy-efficient appliances. Today, we are a trusted name in home appliances, dedicated to enhancing everyday living for families across Southeast Asia.
